How We are Making A Difference

Prayaas is a non-profit organization run by the students of IIIT Allahabad,connecting hundreds of people with a single aim-to lighten up the future of some frisky little kids. Prayaas is not just a teaching program from 5pm-6pm, it has much more to it. It is a continuous effort to illuminate the dark areas near Jhalwa,Prayagraj, some of which have never experienced the light of education before. Prayaas is just a small push in the right direction at the right time. And as you would expect, sometimes even a small push can do wonders!!!

Our Projects

At Prayaas, we conduct a large number of donation drives collecting clothes, shoes and other useful items and distributing them to the ones in need.

Health camps are organized timely by Prayaas in collaboration with health centre of IIITA. Free checkup facility is provided by the doctors where more than 300 children are treated.

Various workshops are conducted to help children learn practically. They also include fun activities like sports,drawing, etc.

Whilst poverty decreases and hunger is becoming less common in the world, subsidised food distribution remains a key social security measure to reduce food deprivation.s

Prayaas offers an excellent environment for learning and makes children feel better to improve their knowledge and skills

Stories Of Inspiration

Swati Maurya, a student of Prayaas, was suffering from acute anaemia and was admitted in Swaroop Rani Hospital, Allahabad for around 9 days. Besides having other complications, her haemoglobin percentage had dropped down to as low as 5.2%. Her family was not in a position to deal with the situation financially. Prayaas helped the family by providing monetary support and blood units, owing to which the girl was able to recover and got discharged. Swati is one of the best students of Prayaas now and has continued to excel in her examinations.

Swati’s treatment:

Aanchal and Swati, two of the brightest minds currently studying in Prayaas were granted admission in Vishnu Bhagwan Public School to continue their studies in a renowned English medium school in Prayagraj. Both the girls, despite having such good academic performance, were not able to excel because of their poor economic backgrounds. Team Prayaas arranged for their admissions and is currently bearing their educational expenses.

Admission of Aanchal and
Swati:

Shilpa Diwakar, a student of Prayaas, completed her schooling in the year 2015 but unfortunately was not able to go for further studies due to the lack of support and poor financial conditions of her family. But her determination did not break over the years. Team Prayaas succeeded in convincing her family and was able to enroll her for B.Sc, Computer Science at K.P College, Allahabad in July, 2019. Besides, we decided to finance her college education and help her in her studies as much as we can.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Prayaas?

+ -

Prayaas is a non-profit organization run by the students of IIIT Allahabad, dedicated to providing education to underprivileged children in Jhalwa, Prayagraj. It is not just a teaching program from 5pm-6pm, but a continuous effort to illuminate the dark areas near Jhalwa.

Who can join Prayaas?

+ -

Anyone who is passionate about education and wants to make a difference in the lives of underprivileged children can join Prayaas. We welcome students from IIIT Allahabad as well as other colleges in Prayagraj.

How can I contribute to Prayaas?

+ -

You can contribute to Prayaas by volunteering your time as a teacher or mentor, donating funds or educational resources, or by spreading awareness about our cause on social media. Visit our website for more details on how you can contribute.

What kind of activities does Prayaas organize?

+ -

Apart from teaching, Prayaas also organizes various extracurricular activities like sports, art and craft, and cultural events for the children. We also conduct awareness campaigns on various social issues like health, hygiene, and environment.
